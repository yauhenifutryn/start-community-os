Methodology & privacy

Statistics are computed from event registrations (Luma), hackathon submissions (Devpost), public GitHub activity for accounts provided by applicants, and licensed professional-profile data. All published figures are aggregates over groups of at least 5 people, including every cross-tabulation; smaller cells are suppressed. Individual highlight quotes appear only with the person's explicit consent and with identifying details generalized. Values marked ~ are estimates. Aggregated statistics are anonymous and are processed under our published privacy policy; no figure in this document can or may be used to make a decision about an identifiable person.

This page uses privacy-preserving, cookieless analytics hosted in the EU.
